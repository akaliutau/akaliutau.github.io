Alphard (aHydrae - [the brightest start](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alphard) in the constellation of Hydra) is a hybrid trading system that bridges the gap between black-box models and rigid rule-based strategies. By presenting market data visually to a multimodal AI agent, it enables direct price-action reasoning without relying on lagging indicators - combining human-like market interpretation with strict quantitative risk controls.

Built as a high-precision Level 2 order book predictor, Alphard fuses the intuitive visual reasoning of VLMs with the unmatched pattern-matching superpowers of LLMs—delivering human-like market interpretation backed by ruthless quantitative risk control.

This approach demonstrated exceptional promise, showing Sharpe 8.3 in day trading, and annualized Sharpe ratio of 1.8.
